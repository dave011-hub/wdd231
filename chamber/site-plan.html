FIXIT — Site Plan & Project Proposal

1. Site Name

Name: David Agbenyegah Bieku

Site Name: FIXIT

Reason: A hub connecting skilled workers with customers in Ghana by location, simplifying finding and hiring tradespeople.

2. Site Purpose

  • Allow skilled workers to create profiles and be discovered by location.
  • Allow customers to post problems and be matched with nearby workers.
  • Reduce time and friction in finding reliable tradespeople in Ghana.

3. Scenarios (Target Users)

Scenario 1 — Customer: Ama, a homeowner in Accra, needs a plumber for a leaking roof. She opens FIXIT, selects “plumbing,” enters her neighborhood, and posts a short problem description. Within 30 minutes several local plumbers appear; she chooses one with good ratings and books a visit.

Scenario 2 — Worker: Kojo, an electrician in Kumasi, registers on FIXIT with his trade, experience, and service area. He receives a notification for a nearby job and accepts or declines the request, building his reputation through ratings.

Scenario 3 — Edge Case: A customer posts a job late at night. FIXIT queues the request and notifies only workers who are available nearby or offers a scheduled morning booking.

5. Typography

Selected Fonts:

  • Headings: Bricolage Grotesque, bold (700)
  • Body text: Libre Baskerville, regular (400)
  • Buttons / Labels: Bricolage Grotesque semi-bold (600)

Usage rules:

  • H1 ≈ 32px, H2 ≈ 24px
  • Body text ≈ 16px
  • Buttons uppercase, bold, high contrast
